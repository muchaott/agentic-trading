## Final Pick

**Pick Strategy 1 with BIL as the best default version.**

Reason: it has better final equity, CAGR, Sharpe, and max drawdown than the
cash variant over the saved 2016-07-08 to 2026-07-07 backtest, with the same
signal tree and the same TQQQ exposure.

Use the cash variant only when operational simplicity or taxable-account
workflow matters more than capturing T-bill ETF yield, especially if the broker
cash sweep already pays a competitive rate.
